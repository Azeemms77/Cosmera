Galaxies

Galaxies are massive systems of stars, gas, and dark matter bound by gravity. Spiral galaxies have rotating arms, while elliptical galaxies are smooth and rounded.

Nebulae

Nebulae are vast clouds of gas and dust in space. They are often regions where new stars are born and can appear colorful due to ionized gases.

Stars

Stars are luminous spheres of plasma powered by nuclear fusion. They appear as bright point sources in astronomical images.

Asteroids

Asteroids are small rocky bodies orbiting the Sun, mostly found in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter.
